🏛️ Corruption in Kenya — Research Analysis

This project analyzes corruption in Kenya, its causes, effects, and impact on national development. It presents a simplified academic breakdown suitable for students and research presentation.

📌 Causes

  • Weak institutional systems
  • Low accountability in public offices
  • Political interference
  • Poverty and unemployment
  • Poor enforcement of laws

📉 Effects

  • Loss of public funds
  • Poor healthcare and education services
  • Slowed economic development
  • Increased inequality
  • Reduced trust in government

🧠 Key Insight

Corruption reduces national development by diverting resources meant for public services into private gain.

🎯 Conclusion: Strong institutions and accountability are key to reducing corruption in Kenya.

📱 Mobile Phones in Campus Life

This project explores the advantages of mobile phones among campus students and how they affect academic performance. It looks at both positive and negative impacts in a balanced way.

✔ Advantages

  • Easy access to learning materials and online libraries
  • Improves communication between students and lecturers
  • Helps in research and assignment completion
  • Supports online learning platforms and e-books

❌ Disadvantages

  • Can cause distraction during study time
  • Encourages procrastination through social media
  • May reduce face-to-face interaction
  • Can lead to poor time management if misused

🎯 Conclusion

Mobile phones can improve academic performance when used responsibly, but misuse can negatively affect concentration and study discipline.
